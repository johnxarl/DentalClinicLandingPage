Our Story

Founded in 1998 by Dr. Jonathan Reyes, Mr. Tooth Dental Clinic began as a small, two-chair practice in the heart of Dental City. Dr. Reyes, a passionate dentist with a vision for patient-centered care, wanted to create a clinic where everyone—from children to seniors—felt comfortable and confident about their dental health.

In the early years, Mr. Tooth quickly gained a reputation for gentle, thorough care and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere. Word of mouth spread, and by 2005, the clinic had expanded to a larger location, adding new technology and a team of skilled dental professionals.

Throughout the 2010s, Mr. Tooth Dental Clinic continued to innovate, introducing digital x-rays, same-day crowns, and clear aligner orthodontics. The clinic became known not only for its advanced treatments but also for its commitment to community outreach—offering free dental checkups at local schools and hosting annual “Smile Days” for families in need.

Today, Mr. Tooth Dental Clinic is a modern, state-of-the-art facility serving thousands of patients each year. Despite its growth, the clinic remains true to Dr. Reyes’s founding values: compassionate care, honest advice, and a dedication to helping every patient achieve their healthiest, happiest smile.

  • 1998 – Clinic founded by Dr. Reyes
  • 2005 – Expanded to a larger location
  • 2010s – Introduced digital x-rays, same-day crowns, clear aligners
  • Today – Serving thousands with advanced, compassionate care
